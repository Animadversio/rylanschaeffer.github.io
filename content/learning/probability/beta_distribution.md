# Beta Distribution

## Definition

The Beta distribution is a two-parameter continuous distribution over the interval $$[0, 1]$$.
Its density is

$$p(x; \alpha, \beta) = \frac{\Gamma(\alpha + \beta)}{\Gamma(\alpha) \Gamma(\beta)} x^{\alpha-1} (1-x)^{\beta-1}$$
